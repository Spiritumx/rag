Wikipedia Title: Normans
The Normans were a people who in the 10th and 11th centuries gave their name to Normandy, a region in France. They were descended from Norse raiders and pirates from Denmark, Iceland and Norway.

Q: Answer the following question.
In what country is Normandy located?
A: France

Wikipedia Title: Computational complexity theory
Computational complexity theory is a branch of the theory of computation that focuses on classifying computational problems according to their inherent difficulty, and relating those classes to each other.

Q: Answer the following question.
What branch of theoretical computer science deals with broadly classifying computational problems by difficulty and class of relationship?
A: Computational complexity theory
