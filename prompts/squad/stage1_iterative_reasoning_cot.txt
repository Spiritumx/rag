Wikipedia Title: Normans
The Normans were a people who in the 10th and 11th centuries gave their name to Normandy, a region in France. They were descended from Norse raiders and pirates from Denmark, Iceland and Norway.

Q: Answer the following question by reasoning step-by-step.
In what country is Normandy located?
A: Normandy is described as a region in France. So the answer is: France.
