Wikipedia Title: Apollo 11
Apollo 11 was the American spaceflight that first landed humans on the Moon. Commander Neil Armstrong and lunar module pilot Buzz Aldrin landed the Apollo Lunar Module Eagle on July 20, 1969.

Q: Answer the following question by reasoning step-by-step.
Who was the first person to walk on the moon?
A: Apollo 11 was the first spaceflight to land humans on the Moon. The commander was Neil Armstrong. So the answer is: Neil Armstrong.
